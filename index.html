Paolo Does Enigmarch

Enigmarch is a daily puzzle-setting challenge, where the organizers release a one-word prompt for people to design a puzzle around. Last year's looked like a lot of fun, so I'm getting a hand on the ball this year! Expect all kinds of stuff, but knowing my tendencies, expect a lot of stuff in the "word-puzzle-leaning hunt-style puzzle with a final answer" sphere. Puzzles will be posted whenever, but ideally within the day. Hope you enjoy! If you're in the mood for other puzzles (mostly crosswords with a few variety puzzles thrown in), check out my website for more.
