Will It Food?

The YouTube show "Good Mythical Morning" has a recurring "Will It...?" segment, which normally entails Rhett and link seeing if foods would taste good when made into other things (see Will it Ice Cream Sandwich? for an early example) Sometimes they break the form with episodes like "Will it Chair?" but normally, it's foods being made into other foods.
